Policies

Please read before booking 🤍

  • I work with gel products only (HEMA-free Gel X system).
  • Please come with bare nails to your appointment, unless you're booked for an infill — notify me beforehand.
  • No walk-ins. All appointments must be pre-booked.
  • I do not do foreign removals or infills over sets not done by me.
  • It is your responsibility to ensure you are not allergic to any gel products. Please notify me beforehand of any sensitivities.
  • A $10 late fee applies if you arrive more than 10 minutes after your appointment time.
  • After 20 minutes, your appointment will be automatically cancelled as I have back-to-back clients.
  • Payment by card or cash only.
  • No bank transfers accepted.
  • No rush to decide on a design — you can decide at the appointment!
  • I don't always have every charm/gem/colour, so if you'd like to replicate a design exactly, please send it to me in advance.
